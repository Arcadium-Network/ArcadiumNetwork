How to Play

1: Invest in the Game

To get started, you will need to buy some ARC3D using ETH. When you invest, a 10% fee is incurred on your Deposit - this contributes to the Dividends earned by already-existing holders.

Dividends are paid to those who hold ARC3D, so you'll want to Buy a decent amount to hold.

The more ARC3D you hold, the larger your "piece of the pie" and the more ETH Dividends you'll earn!


2: Refer Others to Play

Once you have some ARC3D, you can use your Referral Link to bring others in to join the fun! You only need the one link - it's our site, with your wallet address at the end.

When others use your link, you get a bonus to your ETH Dividends! Just the same, they can also refer others with their link - so you should refer with your link fast, far and wide!


3: Compound your Earnings

Once you have a good HODL of ARC3D, you'll want to start Compounding your earnings - that is, rolling them into more ARC3D!

This will have the effect of growing your "slice of the pie", thus earning more Dividends, faster!


4: Sell Out (???)

Is it time to sell your ARC3D? Are you sure? If so, sell them and receive ETH to withdraw to your wallet - then, you're out of the game!


Thank you for Playing! We hope you'll join in again soon!

ARC3D Hourglass

This is a copy of "RainMaker" by Commonwealth.gg - It holds ARC3D, and earns Dividends. When the Floor is raised, the Minimum value of the last token rises - this will grow larger over time, because the Pricefloor can never sell, transfer or destroy its ARC3D, and it can never be withdrawn from by anyone.

Pricefloor

Price Floor

The Price Floor is a copy of "RainMaker" by Commonwealth.gg - It holds ARC3D, and earns Dividends. When the Floor is raised, the Minimum value of the last token rises - this will grow larger over time, because the Pricefloor can never sell, transfer or destroy its ARC3D, and it can never be withdrawn from by anyone.

Anyone can Raise the floor - the only bonus however is guaranteeing a minimum sell value for the last ARC3D token, which helps to ensure game longevity.
