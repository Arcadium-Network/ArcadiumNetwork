F.A.Q

Arcadium D1VS is an Hourglass-style game, meaning as players Buy and Sell D1VS, the price and value incrementally rises and falls.

Additionally, when players Buy and Sell D1VS, a 10% fee is collected and distributed as Dividends to all players.

When you hold D1VS, you are automatically earning Dividends when other players are buying and selling D1VS.

Whenever you'd like, you can Compound your Dividends into more D1VS or Withdraw them as TRX, straight to your wallet.

The best way to earn more D1VS is to Compound the Dividends you earn, and to Refer others to join in!

You'll find a button ready to copy your Referral Link. When others use it to buy D1VS, you'll get a bonus!

If you no longer wish to play Arcadium D1VS, that is 100% okay! All you have to do is Sell all of your D1VS, then Withdraw them from your Dividends.

You will receive the resulting TRX in your wallet, and you're free to go!

How to Play

D1VS & BTTX

To get started, you will need to buy some D1VS (with TRX), BTTX (with BTT) or both. When you invest, a 10% fee is incurred on your Deposit - this contributes to the Dividends earned by already-existing holders.

Dividends are paid to those who hold D1VS, so you'll want to Buy a decent amount to hold.

The more D1VS and BTTX you hold, the larger your "piece of the pie" and the more Dividends you'll earn.



Arcadium Daily

The Arcadium Daily Pool is a great way to ensure you always have a little TRX each day. Every 24 hours, you will receive 3% of your Total Contributions to the Daily Pool, until the pool is empty. Only contributors can earn from the pool, and other Arcadium Network games and dapps will feed the pool in the future, too!

Simply enter an amount of TRX to invest, confirm the transaction, and enjoy the daily earnings!

As soon as you have joined the game, you can use your Referral Link to bring others in to join the fun! You only need the one link - it's our site, with your wallet address at the end.

When others use your link, you get a bonus to your Dividends! Just the same, they can also refer others with their link - so you should refer with your link fast, far and wide! Each game has its own Referral bonuses - one link works for them all!

Once you have a good bag of D1VS and BTTX, you'll want to start Compounding your earnings - that is, rolling them into more D1VS and BTTX!

This will have the effect of growing your "slice of the pie", thus earning more Dividends, faster!

Is it time to sell your D1VS/BTTX? Are you sure? If so, sell them and receive TRX and/or BTT to withdraw to your wallet - then, you're out of the game!

Thank you for Playing! We hope you'll join in again soon!
