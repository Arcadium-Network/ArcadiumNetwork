How to Play

1: Buy D1VS

When you buy D1VS with TRX, a 10% fee is applied to your deposit. The same applies for every other player, on every deposit. This fee makes up part of the "Dividends" which are paid to those who already hold D1VS. As this goes, once you hold D1VS, you too will earn Dividends from other people depositing TRX!

2: Refer Others

As soon as you have some D1VS, you can use your Referral Link to bring others into the game. When they use your link, you get a bonus to your Dividends! Just the same, they can also refer others with their link - so you should refer with your link fast, far and wide!

3: Compound D1VS

Once you have a good bag of D1VS, you'll want to start Compounding your Dividends - that is, rolling them into more D1VS. This will have the effect of growing the size of your "portion of the pie", thus earning more Dividends, faster!

4: Sell D1VS

When it all falls apart on you - when you can't HODL your D1VS anymore, it's okay to SELL them! When you do, you will receive TRX to withdraw. The sale of D1VS is also subject to a 10% fee, which also makes up part of the "Dividends" paid to those who still hold D1VS!

How it Works

Arcadium D1VS is an Hourglass-style game. As players buy and sell D1VS, the price and value incrementally rises and falls.

10% of every Deposit and Withdrawal is collected as a "Stability Fee" which makes up the Dividends that players earn, as D1VS are bought and sold.

When you hold D1VS, you are automatically earning Dividends from other players who are buying and selling D1VS also.
Whenever you'd like, you can Compound your Dividends, which will get your more D1VS!
You can also Withdraw your Dividends as TRX, straight to your wallet.

The best way to earn more D1VS is to Compound the Dividends you earn from others, and to Refer others to join in!

Click or Tap the "Copy" button, and share your Referral Link anywhere you can!
When others use it to buy D1VS, you get a bonus!

If you no longer wish to play Arcadium D1VS, that is 100% okay!
All you have to do is sell all of your D1VS, then Withdraw them from your Dividends.
You will receive the resulting TRX in your wallet, and you're free to go!
