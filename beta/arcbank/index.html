F.A.Q

Arcadium XARC is an Hourglass-style game, meaning as players Buy and Sell XARC, the price and value incrementally rises and falls.

Additionally, when players Buy and Sell XARC, a 10% fee is collected and distributed as Dividends to all players.

When you hold XARC, you are automatically earning Dividends when other players are buying and selling XARC.

Whenever you'd like, you can Compound your Dividends into more XARC or Withdraw them as TRX, straight to your wallet.

The best way to earn more XARC is to Compound the Dividends you earn, and to Refer others to join in!

You'll find a button ready to copy your Referral Link. When others use it to buy XARC, you'll get a bonus!

If you no longer wish to play Arcadium XARC, that is 100% okay! All you have to do is Sell all of your XARC, then Withdraw them from your Dividends.

You will receive the resulting TRX in your wallet, and you're free to go!

How to Play

Dividends are paid to those who hold XARC, so you'll want to Buy a decent amount to hold.

The more XARC you hold, the larger your "piece of the pie" and the more Dividends you'll earn.

As soon as you have some XARC, you can use your Referral Link to bring others into the game.

When they use your link, you get a bonus to your Dividends! Just the same, they can also refer others with their link - so you should refer with your link fast, far and wide!

Once you have a good bag of XARC, you'll want to start Compounding your Dividends - that is, rolling them into more XARC.

This will have the effect of growing the size of your "piece of the pie", thus earning more Dividends, faster!

When it all falls apart on you - when you can't HODL your XARC anymore, it's okay to SELL them!

When you do, you will receive TRX as Dividends to Withdraw.
